User Policy

    Send Deletion Request Email:
    • Send an email from the user's registered email address to gargianmastercut@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
      In the email body, mention that you want to delete your account and provide your mobile number for verification purposes.
    Verification Call:
    • Within three days of receiving your email, our team will call the mobile number provided to verify your identity and confirm the deletion request.
      During the call, our team will ask you to confirm some details to ensure that you are the rightful owner of the account .
    Backend Deletion:
    • After successful verification, our team will proceed to delete your account from our backend systems.
      Once deleted, your account and associated data will be permanently removed from our databases.
Please note:

Make sure to use the same Mobile Number registered with your account when sending the deletion request email. Provide accurate and up-to-date information during the verification call to avoid any delays in processing your request. Once your account is deleted, you will no longer have access to any data or services associated with it. If you have any further questions or need assistance, feel free to contact our support team.
